Audience Test Results

The biggest issue that I'm getting feedback on, is the unclearness of how to proceed about interacting with the project:
1. The users do not know that the projects are not clickable, and they just tried clicking all the time.
2. And they do not know that it is draggable or zoomable at first sight.
3. Lastly, they do not know what compoents are interactable.

The next big thing is the performance issue: the zooming is too laggy to be used normally.

The last is the confusion caused to the unfinished nature of the project. And I think that would striaght itself out when it is fininshed.

The improvements I plan to add is instructional texts for the first issue, but the second one, I am not sure what I can do about it, as the JavaScript code is almost beyond my understanding, optimizing it would be nearly impossible.
